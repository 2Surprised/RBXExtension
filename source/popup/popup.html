Hello world!

Roblox+ is a Chrome extension being developed by @2Surprised on Roblox, and was made in response to a desire to both improve the user experience on the Roblox website, as well as further my knowledge in programming. I hope this extension has afforded you much-needed quality of life improvements!

Options:

Each checkbox represents a feature that is offered by Roblox+. To disable a feature, simply untick its respective checkbox.

Sends native notifications to alert you of friends entering experiences! Stops working when www.roblox.com is closed.

Extends the friend carousel on the home page to make room for up to 50 friends! Reload the webpage to update preferences.

Dragging profile icons into a new tab automatically enlarges them! Made for the server tab on game pages.

Don't want to accidentally lose the people you love? Enable this to prevent unfriending people!

Logging out by accident? Enabling this setting will stop any attempt at logging out!
