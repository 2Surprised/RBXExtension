Hello world!

Roblox+ is a Chrome extension being developed by @2Surprised on Roblox, and was made in response to a desire to both improve the user experience on the Roblox website, as well as further my knowledge in programming. I hope this extension has afforded you much-needed quality of life improvements!

Options:

Each checkbox represents a feature that is offered by Roblox+. To disable a feature, simply untick its respective checkbox.
